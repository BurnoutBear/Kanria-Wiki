Wyvern of Ashenhollow

📂 Bestiary / Dragon-kin 🕯 Updated: March 2026 👁 4,821 views
⚜

The Wyvern of Ashenhollow is a large draconic predator endemic to the volcanic highlands of the Ashenhollow region, northeast of the Cradle Sea. Unlike its true dragon cousins, a Wyvern possesses only two legs and relies on its powerful wings as forelimbs, granting it extraordinary aerial agility at the cost of ground stability.

Description

Adults measure roughly eighteen feet from snout to tail-tip, with a wingspan approaching forty feet. Their scales shift between charcoal grey and deep crimson depending on body temperature — a reliable indicator of imminent breath attack. The eyes are amber-gold and capable of seeing clearly through volcanic ash clouds.

"Do not mistake the silence of its approach for mercy. The Wyvern stills its wings only when it has already chosen you as prey." — Aldric of the Hunters' Compact, Field Notes Vol. III

Behaviour

Wyverns are solitary and fiercely territorial. A single specimen may claim a range of fifty square leagues around its nest-caldera. They are primarily nocturnal hunters, though volcanic activity or encroachment on their territory will provoke daytime aggression. Younger wyverns are far more erratic and dangerous in their aggression than elder specimens, who have learned patience.

Combat

Hunters facing a Wyvern should be prepared for three primary attack patterns:

  • Ash Shroud — exhales a billowing cloud of toxic ash, obscuring vision and poisoning the lungs. Move upwind immediately.
  • Tail Sweep — uses its barbed tail as a flail in a wide arc. The barbs carry paralytic venom; even a graze can immobilise a limb.
  • Dive Strike — climbs to high altitude then folds wings into a near-vertical plunge, striking with full body weight.

Weaknesses

The underbelly scales are significantly thinner than the dorsal armour. Ice-aligned weapons deal bonus damage, and the creature is notably sluggish in cold weather. Its eyes are sensitive to bright light — a Sunburst sigil or Flash Flask will stagger it momentarily, creating an opening.

Loot

Slain Wyverns may yield Ashen Scale (crafting material), Wyvern Barb (alchemical reagent), Ember Heart (rare drop, required for Dragonblood Elixir), and Wyvern Wing Membrane (used in advanced gliding gear).
